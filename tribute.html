Shankar Nag

Shankar Nag was a legendary Indian actor, director, and screenwriter who made a significant contribution to the Kannada film industry. Born on November 9, 1954, Shankar Nag started his career as a theatre artist and later transitioned into films. He acted in over 90 films and directed 14 films during his career.

What I Adore About Shankar Nag

  • His versatility as an actor and director
  • His ability to connect with the audience through his performances
  • His contribution to the Kannada film industry
  • His commitment to social causes
  • His dedication to his craft

Shankar Nag's untimely death in a car accident on September 30, 1990, was a great loss to the film industry. However, his legacy lives on, and he continues to inspire generations of filmmakers and actors.
